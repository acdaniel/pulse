PFPlay.Sprite(params)
Copyright 2011 Paranoid Ferret Productions Sprites are the basic object for moving, animated graphics on the screen. They have an image, the sprite sheet, which is a collection of "frames" that can be played in succession to produce an animation.
Author: Richard Key.
